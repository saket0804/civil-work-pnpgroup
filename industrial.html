Our Industrial Services

Civil Engineering Services

We offer robust civil infrastructure construction such as foundations, equipment bases, substation buildings, control rooms, drainage systems, and compound walls designed for industrial resilience.

Electrical Engineering

Complete HT/LT electrical installations, cabling, panel assembly, substation development, earthing, and automation support (SCADA, PLC).

Mechanical Engineering

Machinery erection, fabrication & installation of pipelines, ducts, chimneys, and tank structures with precision alignment and NDT standards.

Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

End-to-end AMC & daily plant maintenance, condition monitoring, emergency repair handling, utility management (DG sets, boilers, HVACs), and manpower outsourcing.

Interior Fit-outs (Industrial)

Design & execution of administrative buildings, lab interiors, modular workspaces, and control center rooms with acoustic and thermal solutions.

Renovation & Retrofitting

Modernization of aged industrial infrastructure, strengthening of RCC structures, control room revamps, utility upgradation, and safety enhancements.
